Graphs are generated from FHEM log data. The view can be zoomed and the time span can be selected individually. Are you missing a series within your chart? Just change your gplot file in your FHEM server.

Define your timers, send commands, convert FS20 definitions and step through those different views from a sidebar. At the top of this view, you can also change the current server connection.
